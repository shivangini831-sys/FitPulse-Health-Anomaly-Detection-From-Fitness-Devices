Libraries Used & Justification
1️⃣ pandas
pandas>=1.5.0


Why required:

Reading CSV files (read_csv)

Timestamp conversion (to_datetime)

Handling missing values (fillna)

Resampling time-series data to 1-minute intervals

Merging multiple datasets into a single DataFrame

Role in project:
Core library for data ingestion, preprocessing, normalization, and consolidation.

2️⃣ numpy
numpy>=1.23.0


Why required:

Efficient numerical operations

Creating conditional features (e.g., Active / Inactive status)

Handling array-based computations

Role in project:
Supports pandas operations and feature creation during preprocessing.

3️⃣ matplotlib
matplotlib>=3.6.0


Why required:

Visualizing heart rate trends

Plotting steps, calories, and sleep patterns over time

Generating screenshots for milestone submission

Role in project:
Used for time-series visualization and exploratory analysis.

4️⃣ seaborn
seaborn>=0.12.0


Why required:

High-level statistical plots

Cleaner visualization of distributions and correlations

Enhances readability of graphs

Role in project:
Optional but helpful for clean, professional visualizations.
